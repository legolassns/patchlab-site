Custom PVC Patches

Soft PVC rubber moulded in solid colours, with a three-dimensional relief effect. Resistant to water, UV rays and heavy wear.

Technical specifications

  • MaterialSoft PVC, solid colours
  • ResistanceWater, UV rays, heavy wear
  • Effect3D relief, clean-cut edges
  • BackingHook-and-loop or sew-on
  • Minimum orderAssessed per project

When to choose PVC

Suited to garments and equipment exposed to water, friction or harsh weather conditions: outdoor clothing, technical backpacks, operational gear.
