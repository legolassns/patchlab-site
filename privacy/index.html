Privacy Notice

This notice explains how PatchLab handles the data you submit through the quote request form. Last updated: 23 July 2026.

Who we are

PatchLab is a vertical project by Euroricami dedicated exclusively to custom patches. This website is operated as part of Euroricami's activity.

Full legal entity details (registered name, registration number, registered address) for this notice: [DA COMPLETARE DALLA DIREZIONE].

What we collect and why

When you submit the quote request form (Request a Quote), we collect the following:

  • Full name — required
  • Company / organisation — optional
  • Email address — required
  • Phone number — optional
  • Patch type, application type, approximate quantity — patch type and quantity required, application type optional
  • Project description (free text) — optional

We use this information solely to:

  • handle your quote request and assess your project;
  • reply to you and, if needed, ask follow-up questions about the same request;
  • send communications strictly related to the request you submitted.

We do not use this data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose other than handling your request and related communications. We do not carry out automated decision-making or profiling based on the data you submit.

How your data is handled

The form is processed by a server-side endpoint that validates the fields and sends the content by email to a dedicated PatchLab mailbox, using Zoho Mail as the email infrastructure. No database or CRM stores your submission beyond that mailbox at this time.

Cookies and analytics

This website uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect or store any personal data. It counts page views and a small set of on-page interactions (e.g. clicking the "Request a quote" button, submitting the form, switching language) in aggregate form only, without identifying individual visitors.

Nothing you type into the quote request form (name, company, email, phone, patch type, application, quantity, notes) is ever sent to Plausible or to any other measurement tool. The only information recorded about a form submission is whether it succeeded or failed and which language the page was in — never its content.

No cookie consent banner is shown because no cookies are set and no individual is identified or tracked across sites. We do not carry out profiling or behavioural targeting. Last updated: 23 July 2026.

Data retention

Submitted messages are kept in the mailbox for as long as needed to handle your request and any related communication. No automated deletion procedure is currently defined; a formal retention policy is under evaluation. Exact retention periods: [DA COMPLETARE DALLA DIREZIONE].

Depending on applicable data protection law, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data, to object to or restrict its processing, and to request its portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

For any privacy-related question or request, write to info@patchlab.net.
