Embroidered Patches

Continuity

From first order to reorder

Every patch is designed to be reordered over time. Artwork files, measurements, colours, backing type and a photo of the approved sample are all properly archived, so a reorder reproduces the original result exactly, even years later.

  • Measurements and colours
  • Backing type
